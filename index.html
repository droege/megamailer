Welcome to Megamailer!

This is not very sophisticated: It takes a CSV-style set of variables and does a mail-merge on the fields for recipient, subject and email body.

The messages that are generated in this way are passed on to your default email app. Within that app, you'll still have to send each email manually.

This tool is therefore not very useful for really big email campaigns. But for smaller sizes, it should work fine and it allows you to check each email separately.

By the way: This page does not send any of the data you enter here anywhere - it lets your email app do that instead.

Use the sample data to understand how to use the %variables%.
